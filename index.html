About us

It started off by Pat Salmon who lived in Jordan with a great passion to make homemade cookies. This started as a hobby not somthing he wanted to do. But when peopled started to taste the cookies he realized there was a massive potential for his secret recipe. Since then in 1995 he created the company Salmon Cookies and expanded all over the globe.
